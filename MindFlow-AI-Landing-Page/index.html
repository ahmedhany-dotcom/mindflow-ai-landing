FAQ

Common questions

What is MindFlow AI?+
MindFlow is an intelligent workflow automation platform that uses AI to connect your tools, automate tasks, and optimize team productivity. It's designed for modern teams who want to eliminate repetitive work.
How does the free trial work?+
You get 14 days of full access to the Pro plan with no credit card required. At the end of the trial, you can choose a plan or continue with the free Starter tier.
Can I integrate with my existing tools?+
Yes! MindFlow supports 200+ integrations including Slack, Jira, GitHub, Salesforce, HubSpot, Notion, and more. Custom API integrations are also available on Business and Enterprise plans.
Is my data secure?+
Absolutely. We're SOC 2 Type II certified with end-to-end encryption, SSO support, and are GDPR compliant. Enterprise customers also get dedicated infrastructure and custom data residency options.
What happens if I exceed my plan limits?+
We'll notify you when you reach 80% of your plan limits. You can upgrade at any time, and we never stop your workflows — we'll just suggest an upgrade to better fit your needs.

Why we built MindFlow

In 2022, our founding team was frustrated by the endless manual work that consumed their engineering sprints. They built an internal tool to automate task routing — and realized every team had the same problem.

MindFlow was born from that frustration. Today, we help thousands of teams reclaim their time and focus on building great products instead of managing busywork.

Based in San Francisco with a distributed team across 12 countries, we're backed by $18M in Series A funding from Sequoia and a16z.
